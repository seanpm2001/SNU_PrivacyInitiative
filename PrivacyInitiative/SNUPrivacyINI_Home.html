SNU Privacy initiative

Welcome to the SNU privacy initiative. SNU is strongly committed to user privacy. It is getting harder and harder to get privacy these days. We hope to fix this.

This page currently serves as an archive for our articles written on privacy from January 15th 2020 to January 17th 2020. I started the initiative late into SNU site development.

The SNU Privacy Initiative Mission

The goal of the SNU Privacy Initiative is to give awareness to mass-surveillance and privacy invasion, and how deadly and evil it really is. To raise awareness, we can get people to do something to preserve privacy.

Articles are written to spread awareness and get people to take action.

The Favicon of the privacy initiative resembles an eye. This eye resembles surveillance. Notice how the eye has growing cracks on it? This is to symbolize our mission: when people are aware and do something, we can break mass-surveillance, and end privacy invasions on a global scale.
